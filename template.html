Draft Derby

How it works

  • Every night at 6:00 PM, everyone gets a random roll added to their total. Small rolls to start — they get bigger.
  • Lock Week: each night, the lowest total gets locked into the worst open draft slot. Pick 12 goes first. Then 11. Then you.
  • The Finale: the last two standing roll head-to-head for the #1 pick — two days before the draft.
  • Events hit twice a week. The can of surströmming opens for someone. The beer mile giveth.
  • Most points = first pick. No re-rolls. No mercy. Provably rigged-proof (see below).

The commitment

How each roll is made

roll = HMAC-SHA256(seed, year·date·name·purpose) % 20 + 1 — a pure function. Same seed, same date, same name → same number, every time, on anyone's computer. The commissioner can't re-roll, cherry-pick, or "fix a glitch" without changing the seed — which would break the fingerprint above.

The reveal

  1. After the finale, the master seed is published right here.
  2. This page then recomputes every roll of the season in your own browser and checks them against every drop as it was posted.
  3. Extra credit: hash the revealed seed yourself and match the day-one fingerprint.
